About Us

Welcome to Bellies N' Barbells, where we support every stage of pregnancy with strength, wellness, and empowerment. Founded in 2018, a year after my wife gave birth to our beautiful son, Amadou, our gym is the first Black-owned gym in Ottawa that focuses on pregnant individuals, aiming to help them every step of their journey. Inspired by my wife's journey during her first pregnancy and the challenges she faced staying active, I founded this gym to help others in similar positions. We believe that pregnancy should not limit anyone from continuing their passion for fitness and sports.

Our mission is to consistently deliver an experience that makes our members, staff, and guests feel welcome, feel comfortable, and feel healthy. Our established guidelines and policies will optimize every member's health and fitness experience. Additionally, our mission is to make women feel empowered throughout pregnancy by promoting a healthy lifestyle, fitness, growth, support, and mobility.

At Bellies N' Barbells, we offer personalized training, fitness classes, nutrition guidance, and prenatal support to keep you active, healthy, and confident. Our expert team is dedicated to providing the highest quality care and support. More than just a gym, we're a community. As a Black-owned business, we pride ourselves on creating an inclusive environment for all expectant parents, catering to making people who are pregnant comfortable at the gym with no judgment. Whether you're a first-time parent or experienced, you'll find a supportive community here.

Join us at Bellies N' Barbells and experience the difference that strength, support, and community can make in your pregnancy journey. Together, we'll thrive, one workout at a time.
